Biography

At 27 years old, Brandy Norrbom embodies the spirit of versatility and determination. As a devoted mother of two, Brandy juggles family life with a dynamic career in technology and a passion for continuous learning. Her professional journey is rooted at Tillamook Creamery in Oregon, where she plays a crucial role as an IT Field Technician. For nearly two years, she has been instrumental in supporting high-level board operations and providing IT Service Desk services, showcasing her technical acumen and commitment to excellence.

Beyond her responsibilities at Tillamook, Brandy is ambitiously expanding her expertise by studying data analytics at Bellevue University. This endeavor reflects her innate curiosity and desire to forge new paths in the technology domain, particularly in interpreting and narrating the compelling stories hidden within data.

Brandy's zest for life extends beyond her professional and academic pursuits. A lover of travel, writing, and reading, she finds joy and inspiration in exploring new destinations, penning her thoughts, and delving into a diverse range of literature. Her recent travels have taken her to vibrant locales such as San Diego, San Francisco, the majestic Redwoods in Northern California, and the serene landscapes of Kauai. Each journey enriches her perspective, fueling her creativity and broadening her horizons.

Creativity is a core aspect of Brandy's identity. She expresses her artistic side through painting, skillfully using acrylic and watercolor mediums to bring her visions to life. Whether it's a tranquil landscape or an abstract composition, each piece is a testament to her ability to capture emotion and beauty on the canvas.

Her childhood, marked by a deep affinity for nature, literature, and the arts, has profoundly influenced her interests and passions. From her early days of climbing trees and immersing herself in the enchanting worlds of dragons, fairies, and mythology, to her attendance at numerous Shakespeare plays, Brandy's formative years laid the foundation for her diverse interests.

Today, when not immersed in her work or studies, Brandy dedicates her time to her children, her rough collie, and her three chickens, embracing the joys and challenges of motherhood. She also cherishes moments spent in her garden, nurturing life in its myriad forms.

Brandy's journey reflects a remarkable blend of resilience, creativity, and a relentless pursuit of growth. As she continues to navigate the realms of technology and data analytics, her story inspires those around her to embrace their multifaceted talents and to persevere in the face of challenges. With her sights set on furthering her education and honing her craft in writing and painting, Brandy Norrbom stands as a beacon of inspiration, illustrating the boundless potential that lies within the pursuit of one's passions and dreams.

Career & Interests

Brandy has been working as an IT Field Technician for Tillamook for two years. Prior to employment with Tillamook, Brandy spent time as a manager and barista with Starbucks.

She is finishing her Associate's degree with Bellevue University in the spring and also holds her Public Notary and an IT Certification in Desktop and Networking support.
